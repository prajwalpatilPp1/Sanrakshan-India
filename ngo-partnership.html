Foundation Partners

Rainbow Foundation India (RFI) is an Indian NGO that operates the Rainbow Homes programme, in partnership with Sanrakshan. RFI is dedicated to supporting underprivileged and marginalized groups, including children, women, and youth. Sanrakshan receives 60% of its grant support from RFI - https://rainbowhome.in/

The philanthropic arm of Infosys Limited, Infosys Foundation is known for its work in education, healthcare, rural development, arts, and disaster relief. The foundation recently partnered with Sanrakshan to support the comprehensive development of children in its care. Their assistance spans academic enrichment, emotional well-being, physical health, and skill-building—fostering all-round development in a safe, supportive environment. https://www.infosys.com/infosys-foundation/

Smile Foundation India empowers underprivileged children & youth, through education, healthcare, and livelihood programs. We utilize the best methodologies and technology to maximize social return on investment and uphold good governance. They supported us with the part renumeration for the teacher who are working in CCI and RCCLC http://www.smilefoundationindia.org/
