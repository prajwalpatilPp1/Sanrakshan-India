About Sanrakshan

Sanrakshan is a registered national-level non-governmental organization, working in accordance with the Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act, 2015. We are committed to rescuing, caring for, and protecting children from vulnerable backgrounds—including those living on the streets or experiencing abuse, neglect, or other traumatic conditions. Our mission is to provide these children with safety, shelter, education, and holistic development opportunities, enabling them to lead dignified and empowered lives



Our Vision, Mission & Goals

VISION

OUR VISION

A world where every child enjoys their right to life with dignity and a complete childhood — free from discrimination, abuse, violence, exploitation, and neglect.

MISSION

OUR MISSION

To act as a catalyst for sustainable change in the lives of underprivileged and marginalised communities — including children, women, and youth.

GOAL

OUR GOALS

To ensure no child or youth is deprived of their right to family care, quality education, healthcare, and skill training for a dignified life.

Our Key Programs include:

Child Care Institution (CCI)

Providing institutional care and protection to the vulnerable children.


Community-Based Care (CBC)

Supporting children in family-like and community settings.


Community Care Learning Centre (CCLC)

Offering safe learning spaces and life skills education.

Future Program for Young Adults (FPYA)

Vocational training, life skills, and career guidance for independent living.


Special Juvenile Police Unit (SJPU)

Working with the police station law enforcement to safeguard children's rights.

Digital Literacy Programs in Government Schools

Bridging the digital divide by offering computer literacy and access to technology.
