# Requirements Document

## Introduction

This specification addresses the need to make the team section more uniform and professional across the Sanrakshan India website. The current team section has inconsistent styling, layout issues, and lacks professional presentation standards that would enhance the organization's credibility and user experience.

## Glossary

- **Team Section**: The webpage section displaying team member information including photos, names, titles, and contact details
- **Team Card**: Individual component displaying a single team member's information
- **Professional Layout**: Consistent, clean design that follows modern web design principles
- **Uniform Styling**: Consistent visual appearance across all team member cards
- **Responsive Design**: Layout that adapts properly to different screen sizes

## Requirements

### Requirement 1

**User Story:** As a website visitor, I want to see team members displayed in a professional and uniform manner, so that I can easily identify and learn about the organization's staff.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a user visits the team page, THE Team Section SHALL display all team members in identical card layouts
2. WHILE viewing team member cards, THE Team Section SHALL maintain consistent spacing, typography, and visual hierarchy
3. THE Team Section SHALL ensure all team member photos are displayed with uniform dimensions and aspect ratios
4. THE Team Section SHALL apply consistent color schemes and styling across all team cards
5. WHERE team member information varies in length, THE Team Section SHALL maintain uniform card heights through proper content alignment

### Requirement 2

**User Story:** As a website visitor, I want team member information to be clearly readable and well-organized, so that I can quickly understand each person's role and qualifications.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Team Section SHALL display team member names using consistent typography and sizing
2. THE Team Section SHALL present job titles with uniform formatting and positioning
3. WHEN displaying team member photos, THE Team Section SHALL ensure all images are properly cropped and centered
4. THE Team Section SHALL maintain consistent button styling for social media links and bio access
5. THE Team Section SHALL ensure text content is properly aligned and spaced within each card

### Requirement 3

**User Story:** As a website visitor using different devices, I want the team section to display properly on all screen sizes, so that I can access team information regardless of my device.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN viewing on desktop screens, THE Team Section SHALL display team members in a grid layout with appropriate spacing
2. WHEN viewing on tablet devices, THE Team Section SHALL adjust the number of columns while maintaining card uniformity
3. WHEN viewing on mobile devices, THE Team Section SHALL stack team cards vertically with consistent spacing
4. THE Team Section SHALL ensure all interactive elements remain accessible across different screen sizes
5. THE Team Section SHALL maintain professional appearance and readability at all responsive breakpoints
