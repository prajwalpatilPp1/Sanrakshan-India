# Requirements Document

## Introduction

This specification addresses the need to make the legal documents section uniform and consistent with the overall design patterns used across the Sanrakshan India website. The current legal documents page has inconsistent styling, mixed color schemes, and lacks the professional uniformity found in other sections like the team pages.

## Glossary

- **Legal Documents Section**: The webpage displaying all statutory and compliance documents for Sanrakshan India
- **Document Card**: Individual component displaying a category of legal documents with download/view options
- **Uniform Styling**: Consistent visual appearance across all document categories and action buttons
- **Professional Layout**: Clean, organized design that follows the site's established design patterns
- **Document Category**: Grouped collections of related legal documents (Registration & Compliance, Accreditation, Policies)

## Requirements

### Requirement 1

**User Story:** As a website visitor, I want to see legal documents organized in a professional and uniform manner, so that I can easily find and access the documents I need.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a user visits the legal documents page, THE Legal Documents Section SHALL display all document categories in identical card layouts
2. WHILE viewing document cards, THE Legal Documents Section SHALL maintain consistent spacing, typography, and visual hierarchy
3. THE Legal Documents Section SHALL ensure all document category headers use uniform styling and color schemes
4. THE Legal Documents Section SHALL apply consistent button styling for all view and download actions
5. WHERE document lists vary in length, THE Legal Documents Section SHALL maintain uniform card appearance through proper content alignment

### Requirement 2

**User Story:** As a website visitor, I want document information to be clearly readable and well-organized, so that I can quickly identify and access specific legal documents.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Legal Documents Section SHALL display document names using consistent typography and sizing
2. THE Legal Documents Section SHALL present category headers with uniform formatting and brand-consistent colors
3. WHEN displaying document lists, THE Legal Documents Section SHALL ensure consistent spacing and alignment
4. THE Legal Documents Section SHALL maintain uniform button styling for view and download actions across all documents
5. THE Legal Documents Section SHALL ensure proper visual separation between different document categories

### Requirement 3

**User Story:** As a website visitor using different devices, I want the legal documents section to display properly on all screen sizes, so that I can access documents regardless of my device.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN viewing on desktop screens, THE Legal Documents Section SHALL display document cards in an organized grid layout
2. WHEN viewing on tablet devices, THE Legal Documents Section SHALL adjust card layouts while maintaining uniformity
3. WHEN viewing on mobile devices, THE Legal Documents Section SHALL stack document cards vertically with consistent spacing
4. THE Legal Documents Section SHALL ensure all download and view buttons remain accessible across different screen sizes
5. THE Legal Documents Section SHALL maintain professional appearance and readability at all responsive breakpoints

### Requirement 4

**User Story:** As a website visitor, I want the legal documents page to match the visual design of other pages on the site, so that I have a consistent browsing experience.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Legal Documents Section SHALL use the same color palette and brand colors as other site sections
2. THE Legal Documents Section SHALL apply consistent card styling patterns used in team and other sections
3. WHEN displaying headers and titles, THE Legal Documents Section SHALL use the same typography hierarchy as other pages
4. THE Legal Documents Section SHALL maintain the same spacing and layout patterns found throughout the site
5. THE Legal Documents Section SHALL ensure button styles match the design system used across other pages
