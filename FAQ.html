Frequently Asked Questions

At Sanrakshan, we believe every child deserves safety, care, and opportunity. Real change happens when society comes together to protect and empower vulnerable children. By partnering with communities, authorities, and stakeholders, we ensure children grow up with dignity, education, and the skills to lead independent lives.

Make a one-time or recurring donation online, via bank transfer, other authorized channels, or by visiting our Child Care Institutions: Sanrakshan Rainbow Home, Rajajinagar, and Sankalpa Makkala Dhama, Chikkanagamangala. Your support directly helps rescue, care for, and empower vulnerable children.
