What We Do

Every child, no matter their background, deserves safety, education, and opportunity.

Childcare & Development

We provide safe, nurturing environments where children can grow with dignity and access the support they need for holistic development.

  • Sanrakshan Rainbow Home for girls.
  • Sankalpa Makkala Dhama for boys.
  • Community-Based Care (CBC).
  • Community Learning Centre (CLC).

Child Protection

We work in close coordination with the child welfare system and law enforcement to rescue, protect, and support children in hazardous or abusive situations.

  • Special Juvenile Police Unit (SJPU) Program.

Skill Development

Empowering adolescents with the tools they need to transition into independent adulthood through career readiness and life-skills training.

  • Vocational Training & Job Placement.
  • Future Program for Young Adults (FPYA).

Digital Literacy

Bridging the digital divide for children in government schools and underserved communities by setting up smart classroom infrastructure and providing digital learning access.

  • Digital Classrooms Program.

Let's Donate to Needy People for Better Lives

Through your donations, we spread kindness and support to children, families, and communities struggling to find stability.
