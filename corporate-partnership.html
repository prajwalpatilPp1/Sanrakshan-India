CSR Partners

Accel is a globally renowned venture capital firm that backs high-potential startups and entrepreneurs. For the last three years, Accel has been a steadfast partner of Sanrakshan, providing essential support for children’s daily needs—including nutrition, education, healthcare, and holistic development. Their continued support has helped Sanrakshan create a stable, nurturing environment for every child in its care.

Alpha-Elsec is a leading firm in the development and manufacturing of advanced defense electronics, avionics, and satellite systems. For the past three years, Alpha has generously supported Sanrakshan by funding critical child welfare needs such as nutritional supplements, educational costs, and healthcare. The company also financed the purchase of a dedicated vehicle, used to safely transport children for medical appointments, academic activities, and educational excursions.

BorgWarner is a global leader in sustainable mobility solutions. The company supported Sanrakshan Rainbow Home through the installation of a solar energy system. This green energy initiative has significantly reduced dependency on conventional power sources, enhanced energy efficiency, and enabled more funds to be directed toward children's welfare and development.

PlanSource is a provider of cloud-based benefits administration solutions that help organizations manage modern employee benefit programs. Over the past two years, PlanSource has supported Sanrakshan’s children through various initiatives, including educational and nutritional aid. Their financial support has also contributed to the infrastructure development of Sankalpa Makkala Dhama, a dedicated Child Care Institution (CCI) for vulnerable boys.

Sineng Electric is a prominent player in the solar energy sector, providing end-to-end solutions for solar installations. For the past three years, SEIPL has extended generous financial grants to Sanrakshan to support children’s nutrition, education, and healthcare. Their contributions have ensured that children receive a balanced diet, access to quality education, and timely medical attention.
