Digital Classroom Program

Bridging the digital divide for children in government schools and underserved communities. Through our partnership with eVidyaloka, we bring digital classrooms to rural schools, providing students with access to interactive lessons, trained volunteers, and modern learning resources that transform education and create opportunities for success.

Digital Classroom Program

Objectives of the Program

  • Empower Through Education: To create a knowledgeable and empowered India by providing access to quality education for all.
  • Leverage Volunteer Passion: To connect motivated and skilled volunteers with students, ensuring quality educational support, especially for children in rural areas.
  • Bridge the Educational Divide: To reduce disparities in education by offering digital learning opportunities to underserved and rural communities.
  • Enhance School Infrastructure: To support government schools in improving infrastructure and teaching resources, thereby raising the overall standard of education.

Why Need This Program

Many government schools in Karnataka face challenges such as poor infrastructure, lack of basic facilities, and limited access to digital learning tools. Children from marginalized and rural communities are most affected, often missing out on quality education. Poor school conditions and outdated teaching methods can demotivate students, leading to low engagement and higher dropout rates.

To address this, Sanrakshan has partnered with eVidyaloka to bring digital classrooms to government schools. Through this initiative, students gain access to interactive lessons, trained volunteers, and modern learning resources. Combined with efforts to improve school infrastructure, the program aims to create a supportive and motivating environment, helping children continue their education and reach their full potential.

Key Interventions

1. Formal Schooling Support

  • Strengthening education in government schools through strategic partnerships.
  • Improving learning outcomes with structured academic support.
  • Aligning programs with school curricula for maximum impact.

2. Digital Classrooms in Rural Schools

  • Setting up digital classrooms in remote government schools.
  • Volunteers from across the globe teach students for two hours weekly.
  • Lessons delivered in regional language for better understanding.

3. STEM and Digital Literacy

  • Introducing coding and computer literacy skills.
  • STEM-related activities and hands-on learning.
  • Basics of Artificial Intelligence and emerging technologies.

4. Holistic Skill Development

  • Teaching Science, Math, and English effectively.
  • Life skills and leadership development programs.
  • Promoting well-rounded student growth.

5. Volunteer Support & Teaching Aids

  • Comprehensive teaching aids for volunteers.
  • Structured lesson plans and presentations.
  • Multimedia resources for engaging learning sessions.
