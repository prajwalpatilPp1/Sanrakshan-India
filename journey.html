Our Journey

2020
Foundation & Outreach
  • Sanrakshan is a registered non-profit Society focused on the care and protection of vulnerable children.
  • Restoration & resettlement of children from government homes in Lucknow, Kanpur, and Patna.
2021
First Residential Home for Girls
  • Baalika Brundavana — residential care for 30 girls.
  • Established with support from Mr. Pramod Kulkarni, Founder of Sathi Organisation.
2022
Expansion of Care Programs
  • Sanrakshan Rainbow Home for 55 girls. Launched Community-Based Care & Future Program.
  • Opened Community Care Learning Centre in Rayasandra.
2023
Scaling Impact
  • 250+ children reached through CCI, Community-Based Care, Future Programs, and Rainbow Centre with CSR support.
2024
New Initiatives & Partnerships
  • Counselling & Residential Home for Boys. Counselling interventions at 32 police stations (SJPU partnership).
  • Established “Sankalpa Makkala Dhama” for vulnerable boys.
2025
Digital Classroom Program
  • Partnered with eVidyaloka to launch the Digital Classroom Program in government schools.
  • Collaborated with 6 govt schools, enhancing digital infrastructure.
  • Reached 500+ student beneficiaries through technology-enabled learning.
