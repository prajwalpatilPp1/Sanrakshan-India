Childcare & Development

We provide safe, nurturing environments where children can grow with dignity and access the support they need for holistic development. Through Sanrakshan Rainbow Home for Girls and Sankalpa Makkala Dhama for Boys, we rescue children from vulnerable circumstances and offer them comprehensive care, education, and emotional support to help them build brighter futures.

Child Care Institutions

Sanrakshan Rainbow Home

  • A residential home for girls offering care, education, counselling, and life skills.
  • Operating for the past 4 years at the Govt Primary & High School, Jooganahalli, Rajajinagar.
  • Provides shelter, education, medical & counselling support to 60 girls (ages 7–18) from vulnerable backgrounds.

Sankalpa Makkala Dhama

  • A residential home for boys offering care, education, counselling, and life skills.
  • Operating since November 2024 at the Government Lower Primary School, Chikkangamangala.
  • Provides shelter, education, medical & counselling support to 40 boys (ages 7–18) from vulnerable backgrounds.

Objectives of the Program

Rescue and Protect Vulnerable Children ▼
To identify, rescue, and safeguard girl and boy children who are in vulnerable situations, and to provide essential care, protection, and comprehensive support for their holistic development.
Educational Support Through Bridge & Remedial Programs ▼
To conduct bridge courses and remedial classes that facilitate the reintegration of rescued children into formal schooling, and to empower community members through initiatives in education, community health, nutrition, and overall wellbeing.
Ensure Justice & Protection of Child Rights ▼
To uphold and protect the rights of children in need of care and protection by ensuring appropriate follow-up actions, legal support, and interventions that secure their safety and wellbeing.
Holistic Development of Children ▼
To foster the overall physical, emotional, psychological, educational, and social development of every child under the care of the institution.

Why We Need This Program

School Dropout Crisis

Thousands of vulnerable children aged 7–18 face school dropout and poor learning outcomes due to poverty and displacement.

Unsafe Environments

Children in CCIs, migrant families, and economically disadvantaged households experience unsafe living conditions and trauma.

Child Labor & Exploitation

Many children remain out of school, engage in labor, or face exploitation, with limited access to protection and care.

Girls at Risk

Adolescent girls face heightened risk of early marriage, abuse, and limited opportunities for education and development.

Limited Access

Restricted access to transportation, residential care, and safety further limits opportunities for vulnerable children.

Our Solution

Targeted interventions through CCIs ensure educational inclusion, psychosocial support, life skills development, and a safe, nurturing environment.

Key Intervention

1. Rescue and Rehabilitation

  • Identify children, especially girls, in crisis due to trafficking, sexual abuse, child marriage, cyber exploitation, or substance abuse.
  • Ensure their immediate safety through structured rescue operations.
  • Initiate individualized rehabilitation programs addressing physical, emotional, and psychological needs.

2. Safe Shelter & Trauma-Informed Support

  • Provide 24/7 residential care in a secure, nurturing environment.
  • Employ trained caregivers (emphasis on female caregivers) skilled in trauma-informed care and counseling.
  • Offer specialized care for children affected by online grooming, blackmail, or cyberbullying.

3. Education & Life Skills

  • Facilitate school re-enrollment, bridge learning programs, and remedial education.
  • Conduct life skills training to build confidence, digital literacy, and critical thinking.
  • Provide comprehensive sexuality education focused on body awareness, rights, and safe relationships.

4. Health & Nutrition

  • Ensure access to routine healthcare services and mental health support.
  • Provide sexual & reproductive health education, menstrual hygiene management, and counseling.
  • Offer tailored nutrition plans, especially for children recovering from trauma or substance abuse.

5. Community Advocacy

  • Collaborate with parents, community leaders, panchayats, educators, and government authorities on child protection.
  • Raise awareness to prevent child marriage, child labor, cyber crimes, and substance abuse.
  • Promote safe environments for girls online and offline through sensitization programs and advocacy campaigns.

Impact of The Program

Sanrakshan has positively transformed the lives of over 1,000 children through its Child Care Institutions, focusing on child protection, rehabilitation, and empowerment. The program has achieved significant outcomes across multiple areas, including ensuring safety, promoting education, improving health, supporting psychosocial well-being, and facilitating long-term social integration.

Safety & Protection

Rescued children find immediate safety, stable homes, and abuse-free environments.

Health & Well-being

Regular nutrition, healthcare, and counselling address trauma, malnutrition, and mental health.

Education & Skills

Access to formal and non-formal education, life skills, digital literacy, and career guidance.

Personal Growth

Mentoring builds self-confidence, resilience, independence, and awareness of rights.

Social Reintegration

Support beyond 18 years ensures employment readiness, societal integration, and long-term empowerment.

Community Impact

Empowered children positively influence families and promote broader awareness of child rights and gender equality.
