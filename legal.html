Legal & Statutory Documents

Sanrakshan meets all statutory requirements including registration under Societies Act and FCRA (along with a bank account with SBI), registration under the Income Tax Act (including 80G and 12A), registration on the Darpan2 portal of NITI Aayog, and compliance with CSR under the Companies Act 2013. Sanrakshan’s projects closely align with the Indian government’s Children’s Policy 2013, the Samagra Shiksha Abhiyaan, the Integrated Child Protection Scheme (ICPS), the National Education Policy 2020 and the Nipun Campaign for Fundamental Literacy and Numeracy 2022, aiming to provide universal elementary education and social protection, among others benefits to all children.
